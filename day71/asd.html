Database Systems Lab

서울대학교 데이터베이스 시스템 연구실


The primary goal of the Database Systems Lab is to understand the need of data management and scalable strategies for a variety of traditional and emerging database applications. Emphasis is on the development of new indexing and query processing algorithms, disruptive storage paradigms with new storage media, and scale-out platforms for data services.

The research at the DBS Lab covers a wide spectrum of database problems including:

  • Multi-Model Database System
  • High Performance Database Systems and Flash Memory
  • High Performance Big Data Systems and Scalable Solutions
  • Accelerating Database Engines with Flash Memory
  • Transactional Support and Fast Recovery
  • XML/RDF Indexing and Query Processing
  • Data Services in the P2P and Internet Environments
  • Spatial and Time-series Data Management
